About
Clough Enercore

Clough Enercore Limited deliver a full suite of engineering services from concept and feasibility studies, detailed engineering & EPCM-EPC solutions through production optimization, debottlenecking, commissioning and operational readiness.

Clough Enercore brings together Australia’s leading engineering and project management contractor, with Canada’s EPCM service provider Enercore Projects Limited. Clough acquired Calgary based Enercore Projects in October 2015. The united group provides a production focused approach, which aims to maximize the value of client assets across the project lifecycle. Their delivery model is a fresh approach to oil & gas contracting in North America.
