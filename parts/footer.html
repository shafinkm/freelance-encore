About Us


Enercore joined the Clough family in October 2015, and has been established as Clough’s Canadian Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) project delivery company and Clough’s North American Engineering centre of Excellence. Enercore’s production driven solutions, which aims to maximize the value of client assets across the entire asset lifecycle, aligns well with Clough’s full project lifecycle delivery model, enabling the united group to provide a fresh approach to oil & gas contracting in the Canada market and North America.
